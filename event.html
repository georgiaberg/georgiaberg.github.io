I host a LOT of events!

If you're interested in AI Safety I run 2-3 events a week out of Trajectory Labs. You can see our upcoming events here.

Outside of my work, I try to host the following every month:

  • One open invite hangout (this could be a powerpoint night, a game show, a movie night, karaoke, or something else entirely. The vision is that people can hang out in a low-stakes large group environment without needing to spend money)
  • One book club (usually the last Saturday evening of every month) where we meet to discuss 3-5 readings centered around a theme (like "love", "illness" or "wildness").

If you're interested in attending my non-work events you can join this Discord server or subscribe to this mailing list.

I am always open to hearing from people who have access to nice venues or good ideas for events they'd like my help hosting.
